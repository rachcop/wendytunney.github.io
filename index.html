Wendy Tunney, CHHC, RP

Wendy's practice is rooted in corporate leadership, health coaching, energy healing, and holistic practices. She is a Certified Holistic Health Coach and Reiki Practitioner who assists others to connect to the innate wisdom of their body on a physical, mental, emotional and spiritual level. She combines many theories and practices in order to establish a means of treatment to a healthier, more vibrant and balanced life.

As a holistic health coach, she is able to guide clients to live a healthy life in general, not just through their food, but in all areas of their life including their relationships, physical activity, spirituality, careers and to understand their own unique makeup (Bio-individuality) to see what works for them to feel their best.

Wendy is passionate about each client's wellness and has a deep sense of integrity and kindness, intuition, empathy and curiosity. Wendy is passionate about empowering her clients to create the life they want to live.
